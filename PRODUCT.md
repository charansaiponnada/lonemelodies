# Lone Melodies

## Register

brand

## Users

Readers of literary romance and emotional poetry, primarily from Charan's Instagram community (7,000+ followers). They discovered his micro-poetry on social media and want to go deeper. The site visitor arrives at night, on their phone in bed, or during a quiet reflective moment — looking for something that makes them feel seen.

## Product Purpose

Promote *Unspoken Love*, Charan Sai Ponnada's debut novel. Create an emotional bridge between the micro-poetry readers know from Instagram and the full literary work. Collect launch notification signups. The page itself is not just a marketing tool — it's the first chapter of the reading experience.

## Brand Personality

Romantic, warm, vintage. Literary but not academic. Intimate without being saccharine. The site should feel like finding an old handwritten letter pressed between the pages of a secondhand book — personal, weathered, treasured.

## Anti-references

- Modern dark-mode SaaS. Absolutely no glassmorphism, gradient buttons, or over-engineered tech aesthetics.
- Generic romance stock (pink, hearts, cliché wedding-font romantic). This is quiet love, not valentine's day.
- Overdesigned or busy layouts. The quiet is the point. Every element must earn its place.
- Wattpad/amateur aesthetic. No bright accent colors, tacky typography, or cheap textures.

## Design Principles

1. **The quiet is the point.** Space to breathe. Whispered emotions. The page should feel like 3am thoughts, not a billboard.
2. **Literary craft before visual tricks.** Typography and texture do the work. No decorative flourishes that don't carry meaning.
3. **Warmth from within.** The palette should feel enveloping, not cold — like a dimly lit room with a single amber lamp.
4. **Every word earns its place.** Poetic restraint in copy and layout. No restated headings. No filler.
5. **Poetry in structure.** The page rhythm should mirror the book's emotional arc — gentle opening, rising tension, quiet resolution.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Minimum 4.5:1 contrast for body text. Readable font sizes (no 10px body copy). Proper focus states for keyboard navigation. Respect reduced motion preferences.
