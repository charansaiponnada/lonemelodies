About the Book

A love that lived
in silence

Unspoken Love is a journey through one-sided affection, silent pain, innocent hopes, and the unexpected twists that life often throws at us. It's a heart wrapped in fiction, bleeding poetry, stitched together with memories never truly let go of.

You'll walk with Charan through college corridors, rainy bus stops, tech fests, and long silences. There will be betrayals, breakdowns, letters never sent, and love found in the most unexpected corners.

This isn't a fairytale. But it's beautiful.

"She sleeps with dreams in her eyes,
While I stay awake, writing lullabies.
For a girl who never knew,
How beautiful she looks
when the world's silent too."

The Author

Charan Sai Ponnada

Monochromatic soul with a heart as pure as melody.

Charan is a BTech student, builder, and poet who has spent years writing about the things he couldn't say out loud. Under the name lone_melodies, he has built a community of 7,000+ readers who find themselves in his words — the quiet pain, the unspoken love, the 3 AM feelings that have no name.

Unspoken Love is his debut novel — and his most personal work yet.
