Letter
to Her

21 Days. 21 Reels. One Unspoken Love.

A nameless boy. A nameless her. Letters written from a distance, never sent — but finally spoken. A 21-day reel series to launch Unspoken Love to the world, one letter at a time.

21 Letters
3 Weeks
1 Story

Format

One reel per day. Text on screen. Your voice or trending audio. 30–45 seconds.

Tone

Mix of raw diary-like confessions and poetic metaphor. Never too polished.

CTA

End every reel with: "Full story in Unspoken Love — link in bio 🌿"

Hook

First 2 lines = the hook. Make it hit before they can scroll away.

Dear her,

I don't know when it started. I only know that one morning the world looked ordinary — and then you walked through it, and it hasn't looked ordinary since.
Caption: some people walk into your life quietly. and wreck it beautifully. 🌿 #UnspokenLove

Posting Tips

HOW TO MAKE EACH REEL HIT

01
The First 2 Lines are Everything
Use the hook line as your opening text on screen. Big. Italic. Centered. If it doesn't stop the scroll in 1 second, it won't work. Every hook above is designed to stop thumbs mid-air.
02
Audio Choice
Use slow, melancholic instrumentals — Arijit Singh piano covers, Lo-fi Indian beats, or trending sad audio. Your voice reading the letter over music hits hardest. Try recording in one breath, no pauses.
03
Visual Style
Dark background. White or cream serif text. No fancy transitions — just slow fades. Add a dried flower, an old notebook, or a rainy window in the background. Keep it feeling handwritten, not digital.
04
Caption Formula
Short caption from the card above + 3–4 hashtags + "Full story in Unspoken Love — link in bio 🌿". Don't over-explain. Let the reel do the work. The caption is just the whisper after the scream.
05
Story Continuity
Start each reel with "Letter #[day]" on screen. It creates a series feel — people will come back daily to follow the story. By Day 7 your regulars will be invested in the nameless boy and his unnamed her.
06
Day 21 is the Launch
Save your biggest push for Day 21. That's your book announcement reel. All 20 days before it are warm-up. By Day 21, your audience will be emotionally ready to buy — or at least to share.
